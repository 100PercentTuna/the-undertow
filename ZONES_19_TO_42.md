**Ethiopia, Eritrea, Djibouti, Somalia, Somaliland**

### Key Dynamics
- Post-Tigray War Ethiopia: fragile peace, Amhara insurgency, Oromia tensions, Abiy's trajectory
- Eritrea's hermit kingdom and regional spoiler role (Isaias Afwerki, power since 1993)

- Djibouti as military base hub (US, China, France, Japan, Italy)
- Red Sea security dynamics (Houthi attacks on shipping)
